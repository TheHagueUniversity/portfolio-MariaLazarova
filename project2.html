About the project

Designing a gift giving experience for people living far away from friends and family that expresses the giver’s emotional message. Nowadays we see more and more intangible gifts because of the increase in digital products and a growing interest in experiences.

The giver uses a personalised card to send a caring, supportive and emotional message. And to announce there is an intangible gift waiting for the receiver to be unwrapped. Because the gift is intangible, the unwrapping is digital. This means there are three things that were created for this project that together make up the gift giving experience:

  • Card Personalisation tool
  • Personalised card
  • Digital Unwrapping
